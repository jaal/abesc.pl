Our niche: producing and manufacturing sports clothing and cycle clothing

Get acquainted with the offer of our company as a producer of sport and cycle clothing!

Through a long-term cooperation with our customers and contractors, we have developed our niche market. We specialize in making sports clothing and cycle clothing. We have been providing many reputable companies with our services, such as:

  • Newline
  • Asics
  • Alpinus
  • Craft
  • Giant
  • Campagnolo
  • and other

We have gained experience and skills in producing sports and cycle clothing, such as:

  • cycling jerseys
  • long sleeve jerseys
  • cycling jackets
  • cycling shorts
  • cycling knickers
  • and many others

We do have practical knowledge how to sew from variety of the materials, such as:

  • with waterproof fabric
  • polar fabric
  • wind block and windstopper fabric
  • lycra
  • and other

Our strengths are mainly:

  • many years of experience in manufacturing sport and cycle clothing for many reputable companies from Poland and abroad
  • convenient ways of payment and negotiable prices
  • modern and specialized machine park, which allows us to the complete any kind of order at the highest level of quality

If you are interested in our services, feel free to contact us!
